Animals

Tiger

Tigers (Panthera tigris) are the largest cats, powerful apex predators known for orange fur with black stripes, solitary habits, and hunting large prey like deer in Asian forests, though they are Endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.
Key traits include unique stripe patterns, excellent vision, powerful jaws, and an unusual love for water, making them strong swimmers.

dog

Dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) are domesticated mammals, descended from wolves, known for loyalty and companionship, with over 400 breeds varying greatly in size, looks, and temperament, serving roles from pets to service animals due to their intelligence, keen senses (especially smell), and adaptability.
They're characterized by paws, a coat, and unique traits like wet noses that help them smell, requiring proper care, exercise, and training.

lion

The lion (Panthera leo) is a large cat of the genus Panthera, currently ranging only in Sub-Saharan Africa and India.
It has a muscular, broad-chested body; a short, rounded head; round ears; and a dark, hairy tuft at the tip of its tail.
It is sexually dimorphic; adult male lions are larger than females and have a more prominent mane that usually obscures the ears and extends to the shoulders.
